WUNDER

Wandering Star

Artist:
Jaclyn Mednicov
Year:
2015
Media:
Single-channel video and speakers
Statement:
The making of Wandering Star begins with instinctual video documentation taken while walking through the aftermath of a snowstorm. The original footage is cut, manipulated, and distorted. Using color, texture, and space through addition and subtraction, Wandering Star is meant to be at once familiar and unfamiliar.
